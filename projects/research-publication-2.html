Foundation Models for Logistics Operations

Yunhao Yang, Neel P. Bhatt, Christian Ellis, Samuel Li, Alvaro Velasquez, Zhangyang Wang, Ufuk Topcu

NeuS 2026

Paper Code

Summary

We propose a framework that combines the flexibility of vision-language foundation models with the reliability of formal logistics planners. The system estimates when a user's intent is unclear, asks for clarification when needed, and uses those confidence signals to tune the model toward the intended task.

My role

My contribution focused on the demo and evaluation codebase for the airlift simulator, connecting prompt clarification, intent classification, database updates, planner execution, and generated solution artifacts.
